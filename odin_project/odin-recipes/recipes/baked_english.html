Baked Full English-sytle Breakfast

Description

A healthier spin on a Great British Classic! Bake your your breakfast to reduce the added fat, to create this hearty meal that can really be eaten at any time of day. Feel free to mix and match your selection of ingredients on this one.

Ingredients

  • Bacon (streaky or back)
  • Sausages
  • Black Pudding
  • Eggs
  • Tomato
  • Mushrooms
  • A drop or two of Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180 degrees celsius with a large baking tray inside to warm up at the same time.
  2. Once your tray is preheated, take from the oven and place your selection of Sausages, Bacon, Black Pudding, Tomato (sliced in half) and Mushrooms artfully in the tray. Don't forget to leave space for your eggs to go in later!
  3. Drizzle just the tiniest amount of olive oil over the Black Pudding, Tomato and Mushrooms, add a pinch of salt and pepper too if you'd prefer.
  4. Place your hot and loaded baking tray back in the oven for 20 minutes.
  5. After 20 minutes remove your tray from the overn and promptly crack your eggs into the space you left earlier.
  6. Place your tray back into the oven for a final 5-7 minutes. This timing will depend on how soft you'd like your eggs
  7. Serve and Enjoy!
