Shakshuka

Description

Make this easy Shakshuka for a healthy, filling and nutritious breakfast, brunch or lunch. For extra indulgence, how about adding a large handful of quality grated cheese on top and grilling for a couple of minutes before serving?

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 onion (red or white), finely chopped
  • 1 chilli (red or green - and your preference of heat), finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, sliced
  • Small sprig of coriander, chopped
  • 1 can of chopped tomato
  • 2 or 3 eggs

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a frying pan that has a lid.
  2. Add the onions, chilli, garlic and coriander stalks and lightly fry for 5 mins until soft.
  3. Stir in the tomatoes, then bubble for 8-10 mins until thick.
  4. Using the back of a large spoon, make 2-3 dips (depending on the number of eggs) in the sauce, then crack an egg into each one.
  5. Put a lid on the pan, then cook over a low heat for 6-8 mins, until the eggs are done to your liking.
  6. Scatter with the coriander leaves and serve with crusty bread.
